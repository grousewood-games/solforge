Assets & Concepts ​

  • All game rules, card art, card names & text, dog bark battle sound, and the vast majority of the UI art assets is the creation and intellectual property of Stoneblade Entertainment.
  • The fancy font, Cinzel, was created by Natanael Gama.
  • The green arrows were made by AngryMeteor.
  • The rank orb and animations for card leveling, overload, battle damage, rankup are by Cethiel.
  • The player damage animation is by Sinestesia.
  • The plus and minus icons in the deckbuilder are by Pauliuw.
  • Square icons generated on Game-Icons.
  • Enhanced versions of the large card frames are courtesy of Spruce.
  • OffscreenCanvas polyfill written by Laurin Quast.

Rules ​

A good chunk of the rules documentation was influenced (or outright lifted) from the Solforge Wiki, primarily written by Vandergus, Djurre, and RavenBlackFeather. I'm also pulling content from N3Rk's Guide to Keywords and Mechanics, originally found on the now-defunct Ghox's Socks website.

Gratitude ​

Very grateful to Stoneblade for their permissiveness in letting me share this tribute to the original game. They were certainly not obligated to.

A thank you to Ximane who wrote and published the "Advanced Solforge Rules" back in the day. It has been an indispensable guide in my attempts to recreate the game as accurately as possible.

Much respect to Kaelari for his generous sharing of some original game assets.

Hat tip to Github for the free hosting, and Photopea for the image editing trickery.
